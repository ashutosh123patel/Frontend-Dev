Understanding Web Typography

Typography plays an essential role in creating readable and visually appealing webpages. Good typography improves the user experience and helps readers understand content comfortably.

This article demonstrates the use of different font weights, italics, and various highlighted text styles. Proper use of line-height, letter-spacing, and alignment helps maintain a pleasant reading rhythm.

When designing text-heavy webpages, always consider clarity, balance, and structure. A strong typographic system guides the reader naturally through the content and enhances overall comprehension.
